What we do

Bringing Talent and Opportunity Together

Link Asia Manpower Solutions Corp. (LAMS) is a DMW-licensed land-based recruitment agency connecting global employers with exceptional Filipino professionals. With successful placements across healthcare, engineering, and skilled trades, we are proud to be part of the Filipino dream.

We are on a mission to empower Filipino talent by connecting them with global opportunities, while building lasting partnerships with international employers through integrity, excellence, and an unwavering commitment to success.
